🌌 Welcome to Planet Zorbak

Explore the wonders of Zorbak — the glowing blue world in the Andromeda Sector. Home to shimmering landscapes, mysterious lifeforms, and breathtaking alien technology.

🛰️ Planet Facts

  • Galaxy: Andromeda
  • Atmosphere: Neon Vapor
  • Average Temperature: -80°C to 60°C
  • Day Length: 40 Earth hours
  • Gravity: 1.5× Earth's gravity

👽 Native Species

The Zorblins are the primary intelligent species. These luminous beings communicate using pulses of colored light and live in sprawling crystal hives buried beneath the surface.

🌋 Places to Explore

  1. The Lava Ice Canyons – A stunning blend of molten rock and frozen neon rivers.
  2. Bioluminescent Forests of Krayla – A glowing jungle full of floating plants and whispering spores.
  3. Floating Markets of Sky Ridge – Merchants trade rare minerals and alien tech on sky islands suspended by anti-grav fields.
